About Us

YACS is an open-source course scheduler developed at the Rensselear Center for Open Source at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute.

Our site is located at https://yacs.cs.rpi.edu, and it has its own About page with some more information about our history, goals, and developers past and present.

To get in contact with us, you can email us at yacsrpi@gmail.com or submit an issue or bug report on GitHub.
